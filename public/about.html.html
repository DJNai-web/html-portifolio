About Nai

20+ years of career, this Rio de Janeiro native presents productions that cover all aspects of Drum'n'Bass.

His music has been released by Brazilian as well as international dnb labels.

In addition, shared the decks with big names such as L Side, BTK, Dj Andy, Acuna, Dunk...

From more melodic songs filled with Brazilian influences to the darker and heavier side that 174 bpm has to offer.
